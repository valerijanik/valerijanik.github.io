Internship at Next Games

This is about my time spent at Next Games as a Game Programming Trainee

Tech

Unity and C#, Git, Azure.

Role

Game Programming Trainee

My first work place at a professional game company

My last work place has been at Next Games, a mobile game company, developing games based on franchise entertainment. I was lucky to become one of the students of Next Games Academy, that started in October 2019.

First two months we've been introduced to the industry as a whole and Next Games itself, how the company works, what Tech the company uses and why, had lectures and talks by amazing developers, artists, testers, designers and producers of the company about the ways of working at Next Games. We have learned about the development and the work flow of games companies.

Next four months I've been lucky to be working on the game project, that I was extremely excited - game based on Netflix's series Stranger Things. The team of amazing professionals, who were always ready to talk, explain and help. During this time I've developed several features that we now in the game. I've learned Git more deeply, learned about design and programming as a whole, the process of game development and publishing.

This six months of my time at Next Games I've became a proper Junior Game Developer, who has faced many issues, was able to solve them, who can develop features independently, who was able to look at who systems and add features on top on them.
