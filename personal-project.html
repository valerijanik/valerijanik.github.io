Something I do in my free time

It is normal for me to do my own projects from time to time to develop my programming skills.

Back in 2018-2019 I've created several game prototypes, such as Snake, Flappy Bird, Tetris, some match-3 game, tanks etc.

Now it came to the point when it's not enough create just prototypes, so me and my husband, who is a 3D artist, decided to work on our own mobile game and then we're planning to publish it in Android store. Right now it is in development and will be, hopefully, available in a month or so.

This is meant to be a bullet-hell shooter, where you controll the turret and can buy upgrades. At this moment we're working on an endless mode, where enemies are being spawned constantly with increased speed and time between spawning is getting shorter.
