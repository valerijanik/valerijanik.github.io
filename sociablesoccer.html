My experience at Combo Breaker

I've started working at Combo Breaker, a small game company, who has brought to life Sociable Soccer, a multi-platform game available in Apple Arcade, Google Appstore, for PC and Mac.

I've closely worked with Game Designers, Lead Programmer and Art Director, working on UI features implementation, making menus look consistent throught all game modes, options and all other menu screens.

Mostly I've worked with with Jon Hare and Steven Huckle, the game designers from Tower Studios, who are the publisher of the game, discussing what and how should be implemented as well as discussing the flow of the game.

I've learned a lot about working in a small team, being always opened for a discussion, as well as coming up with creative ways of dealing with issues and bugs.

I've joined Combo Breaker in May, just before a big update, so it's been stressfull several months working on the game. After an update I started working on redisign the website of Sociable Socceer, that can be found here: Sociable Soccer website (currently in development) [upd 29.07.2020]
